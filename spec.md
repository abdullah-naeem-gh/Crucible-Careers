## What is Crucible Careers?

Crucible Careers is a two-sided recruitment platform that merges three things teams usually stitch together with separate tools: a **job board**, a **profile-building platform**, and a full **Applicant Tracking System (ATS)**.

For **job seekers**, it's a single place to build one rich, verifiable profile, pulling in GitHub activity, LinkedIn data, and resume content, instead of re-typing the same information into every application form. That profile can grow over time through badges (e.g., a "Certified AI Engineer" badge earned via a proctored exam), and it gives seekers visibility into how they stack up against other applicants for a role, not just whether they were rejected.

For **recruiters**, Crucible replaces two separate habits companies currently rely on: (1) using Google Forms or spreadsheets to collect applications, and (2) using a paid ATS like Greenhouse or Lever to manage the pipeline. In Crucible, a recruiter builds a custom application form, gets a public shareable link (no login wall to view/share, Google login only to apply), and from there manages the entire pipeline, scoring, filtering, shortlisting, emailing, and even querying an AI chatbot about specific candidates, inside one dashboard.

In short: **Crucible is positioned as a unified hiring layer,** a Google Forms replacement, a LinkedIn-style profile layer, and an enterprise ATS, combined into one in-house product.

## 1. Job Seeker Role

### Core Features (as defined)

- Build a single profile that represents the user fully
- Connect and import data from **GitHub**
- Pull information directly from **LinkedIn**
- Upload and paste a **resume** directly into the profile
- Manually add **projects** and **work experience**
- Earn **badges** on the profile (e.g., "Certified AI Engineer") after passing proctored exams
- **Explore jobs** with filters: location, company, role, and more
- **Explore company profiles** — view a company's page and browse all of its open postings in one place
- See **where they stand among other applicants** for a given role (competitive positioning)
- AI chatbot to help with applying for relevant jobs that fit the experience and requirements.

---

## 2. Recruiter Role

### Core Features (as defined)

- Build a **company profile page** (overview, culture, info) that job seekers can view publicly
- Upload job posts and create a **custom job application form**
- Generate a **public, shareable Crucible link** — usable by anyone, no Crucible account needed to view
- Applicants **log in with Google** to fill out and submit the form
- **Recruiter dashboard** with applicant metrics
- **Quick shortlisting metrics** per applicant:
    - GitHub and related platforms like Dribble, Behance and Google Drive link to see contribution activity
    - Consistency score between LinkedIn, resume, and submitted profile
    - **ATS score** of the resume against the Job Description (JD)
- **Filters** on the applicant list to shortlist faster
- **Automated emails** to shortlisted or rejected candidates, with customizable email templates/formats
- **AI chatbot** for recruiters — ask natural-language questions about a specific applicant's profile, backed by a **vector database** of uploaded profiles
